Denge Sound Mirrors

On Sunday the 19th of July we'll be embarking on the first Infrastructure Club Field Trip to visit Denge Sound Mirrors on their annual open day. We're also going to visit the Leas Lift water-powered funicular railway in Folkestone and have a nice sit on the beach.

Unfortunately our minibus is now completely full and we can't take any more bookings, however if you wish to make your own way to Lydd-on-sea and join us you're more than welcome. We expect to be at the sound mirrors from 12:40-14:00, and the Leas Lift around 14:40.
